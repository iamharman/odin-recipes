Oatmeal Raisin Cookies

Description

Delicious Oatmeal Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 3/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 3/4 cups rolled oats
  • 1 cup raisins.

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 190 degrees C
  2. In large bowl, cream together butter, white sugar, and brown suagr until smooth. Beat in the eggs and vanilla until fluffy. Stir together flour, baking soda, cinnamon and salt. Gradually beat into butter mixture. Stir in oats and raisins. Drop by teaspoonful onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  3. Bake 8 to 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until golden brown. Cool slightly, remove from the sheet to wire rack. Cool completely.
